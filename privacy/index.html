Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

Discipler is built to support mentorship within churches and ministries. We take your privacy seriously and keep things simple and transparent.

01

What we collect

  • Name, email, and phone number
  • Availability and mentorship preferences
  • Basic profile information (interests, goals, etc.)
  • Mentorship activity (meeting check-ins, responses)

02

How we use your information

  • Match mentors and mentees
  • Send reminders and check-ins (including SMS)
  • Help ministry leaders understand engagement
  • Improve the platform experience

03

SMS communication

  • You may receive text messages related to mentorship
  • Examples: meeting reminders, check-ins, follow-ups
  • You only receive messages if you provide your number and opt in

04

Who sees your information

  • Your ministry leaders and administrators
  • Discipler (to operate the platform)
  • Trusted service providers (like SMS delivery)

05

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not send spam
  • We do not share your information outside your ministry context

06

Your control

  • You can request to update or delete your information at any time
  • You can opt out of messages at any time
