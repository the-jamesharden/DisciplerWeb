The problem

Pastors care deeply.
But care alone doesn't scale.

As Gen Z church attendance doubles and ministries grow, demand for mentorship is outpacing the relational infrastructure to support it. Across every denomination, we heard the same three challenges.

Pairing mentors & mentees

Pairing people well requires knowing availability, personality, spiritual maturity, and goals. Without a system, it falls through the cracks or defaults to who the pastor happens to know.

Maintaining accountability

Mentorship relationships stall when there's no structure. Leaders need visibility into whether meetings are happening — without micromanaging every pair.

Collecting feedback data

How do you know if your discipleship program is working? Most ministries have no way to collect meeting notes, track spiritual growth, or report on outcomes at scale.

The solution

Built around how
ministry actually works

Discipler isn't a generic CRM dressed up for churches. Every feature was designed from conversations with pastors who lived the problem — not assumptions made in a startup office.

01

Structured Onboarding

Guide both mentors and mentees through a thoughtful intake process that surfaces what you need to make great pairs — goals, availability, spiritual background, and communication style.

02

Intelligent Pairing

Move beyond guesswork. Discipler surfaces compatible pairs based on real criteria, so ministry leaders spend less time coordinating and more time shepherding.

03

Scheduling Workflows

Built-in scheduling tools help mentors and mentees find time together — with automated reminders that keep relationships moving forward without a pastor chasing everyone down.

04

Discipleship Dashboard

Get a real-time view of your entire discipleship program. See which pairs are meeting, which are stalling, and where your attention is needed — at a glance.

05

Meeting Feedback

After every meeting, mentors and mentees complete a simple check-in. Over time, this creates a rich picture of growth — and gives leaders data to strengthen their programs.

06

Ministry Reporting

When it's time to share results with elder boards, donors, or denominational leadership, Discipler gives you the numbers to show your discipleship program is working.

How it works

Simple to run.
Built to scale.

I

Onboard your community

Mentors and mentees complete a guided intake form. Takes under 10 minutes. No technical setup required.

II

Review suggested pairs

Discipler surfaces compatible pairs. Leaders review and confirm — keeping human judgment at the center.

III

Relationships begin

Pairs schedule their first meeting inside the platform. Automated workflows keep momentum going.

IV

Track & improve

Feedback flows in after each meeting. Your dashboard shows what's working and where to focus.
